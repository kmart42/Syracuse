The asymptotic relationship between f(x)=\(x^2\) and g(x)=\(2^x\) is as follows:\\
L = lim \(f/g\) = \(x^2/2^x\)\\
 '= lim \(2^x/ln2*2^x\)\\
 '= lim \(2/(ln2)^2*2^x\)\\
 '= 0\\
 Because L = 0, f(x) = o(g(x)) and f(x) = O(g(x))
