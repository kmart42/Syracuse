\begin{enumerate}
  \item Over $n$ increments, we flip the first bit $n$ times
  \item We flip the second bit at most $n/2$ times
  \item We flip the $k^{th}$ bit $n/k^{k-1}$ times
  \item The toal running time is just $O(n)$ 
\end{enumerate}
